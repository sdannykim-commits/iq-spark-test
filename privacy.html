Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 14, 2026

IQ Spark ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

Quick Summary: We collect information you provide and usage data to deliver our IQ testing service. We do not sell your personal data. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

Information you provide directly:

  • Contact Information: Email address
  • Account Data: Name (optional), age range
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed by third-party providers)
  • Test Responses: Your answers to assessment questions

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Data Type Description
Device Information Device type, operating system, browser type, screen resolution
Usage Data Pages visited, time spent, features used, test completion rates
Log Data IP address, access times, referring URLs, error logs
Location Data Country and general region (derived from IP address)

1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for Service functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand usage patterns and improve our Service
  • Preference Cookies: To remember your settings and choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect Service functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Purposes

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our Service
  • Process your test and generate results
  • Process payments and send transaction confirmations
  • Send results and reports to your email
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests

2.2 Secondary Purposes

  • Improve and optimize our Service and algorithms
  • Conduct research and analysis (using anonymized data)
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Send promotional communications (with your consent)

3.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share data with:

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights and safety.

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

3.4 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States. These countries may have different data protection laws.

For transfers from the EEA/UK, we use:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit; AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Access Controls: Role-based access limitations for personnel
  • Monitoring: Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Payment Security: PCI-DSS compliant payment processing

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Privacy Rights

7.1 Rights for All Users

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

7.2 Additional Rights for EEA/UK Residents (GDPR)

  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Restriction: Request limitation of processing
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge Complaint: File a complaint with your supervisory authority

7.3 California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

7.4 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@iqspark.com. We will respond within 30 days (or as required by applicable law).

Users aged 13-18 must have parental consent to use the Service.
